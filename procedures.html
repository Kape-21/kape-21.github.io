Procedures

Spine Procedures

A structured overview of minimally invasive, fusion, disc replacement, and deformity correction procedures.

Minimally Invasive Spine Procedures

Minimally Invasive Discectomy

Removal of herniated disc material through a small incision, preserving muscle and soft tissue to enable faster recovery.

Microlumbar Decompression

Targeted decompression of nerve roots to relieve leg pain and numbness caused by spinal stenosis.

Spinal Fusion Procedures

Posterior Lumbar Fusion

Fusion of lumbar vertebrae to stabilize instability, deformity, or severe degeneration.

Cervical Fusion

Fusion of cervical segments to relieve spinal cord or nerve root compression in the neck.

Disc Replacement & Motion Preservation

Cervical Disc Replacement

Replacement of diseased cervical discs with artificial implants designed to preserve neck motion.

Lumbar Disc Replacement

Motion-preserving alternative to fusion in select patients with lumbar disc disease.

Deformity Correction & Trauma

Scoliosis & Kyphosis Correction

Surgical correction of spinal deformities to restore alignment, balance, and function.

Spinal Trauma & Fracture Fixation

Stabilization of traumatic spinal fractures to protect the spinal cord and nerves.
